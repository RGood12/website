Business Process Fundamentals Training: Workday

Description

The Workday business process framework consists of a powerful set of tools that enable automation of business processes and to deliver the right information to the right people, at the right time. Through a series of demos and hands-on activities, this course introduces you to the fundamentals of business process configuration and administration to support the successful deployment and ongoing management of business processes.

This course covers the following topics:

  • Business Process definition configuration
  • Adding condition rules to apply further logic to business processes
  • Using notifications, documents, and reports in business process transactions
  • Configuration of Business Process security to support defined roles and tasks
  • Testing and troubleshooting of Business Processes
  • Managing Business Process events as an administrator
